About Me

    Currently, I am a Ph.D. candidate at in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, IIT Guwahati, India, working with Dr. Vijaya Saradhi Vedula. My dissertation research is focused on mining strengths and weaknesses of cricket players using the live ball-by-ball text commentary data available in commercial sports websites such as EspnCricInfo.

    And before that, I was a M.Tech candidate in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, IIT Guwahati, India, working with Dr. Vijaya Saradhi Vedula. I worked on a convex optimisation problem - `spectral clustering using convex and constrained settings'.
